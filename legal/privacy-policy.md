# Privacy Policy — Palmi

**Last Updated: May 8, 2026**

## 1. Information We Collect

### Palm Images
- We process palm images solely for generating your reading
- Images are **NOT stored** after analysis is complete
- Images are transmitted securely via HTTPS encryption

### Account Data
- Anonymous user identifier (UUID)
- Subscription status
- Reading history (text only, no images)

### Analytics Data
- App usage events (screen views, feature usage)
- Device type and OS version
- No personally identifiable information is collected

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- Generate personalized palm readings
- Process subscription payments (via Apple)
- Improve app performance and user experience
- We do **NOT** sell your data to third parties

## 3. Third-Party Services

- **Apple**: Payment processing for subscriptions
- **RevenueCat**: Subscription management
- **OpenAI**: Palm image analysis (images are not stored by OpenAI per their API data usage policy)
- **DeepSeek**: Text generation for readings
- **Cloudflare**: API hosting and data storage

## 4. Data Retention

- Reading history: Retained until you delete your account
- Palm images: Deleted immediately after analysis
- Analytics: Anonymized and retained for 90 days

## 5. Your Rights

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data at any time
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of all your data
- **Portability**: Export your reading history

Contact: privacy@getpalmi.com

## 6. Children's Privacy

Palmi is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website.

## 8. Contact

For privacy inquiries: privacy@getpalmi.com
